## Project Overview & Goals

The Club Hale website is being developed as a final project for the ICS 314 course at UH Mānoa.  

There are over 200 registered independent organizations (RIOs) at UH Mānoa, along with dozens of informal, active student groups. Despite this rich campus life, finding the right organization can be frustrating. Information is scattered across social media accounts, outdated flyers, and static spreadsheets.  

In Hawaiian, *Hale* means "House". As such, the goal of Club Hale is to bridge the gap between students and student organizations. We want to build a central hub that makes it as easy as possible for students to find organizations that suit their interests through a single, interactive, and searchable directory.  

Whether you're looking to build your resume, pick up a new athletic hobby, play music, or simply find your community on campus, this platform ensures you never miss out on what UH Mānoa has to offer.

## Planned Features

Once fully developed, the platform will deliver a seamless experience tailored to students, club leaders, and system administrators.

### 1. Centralized Club Directory

* **Detailed Profiles:** Every club listing includes descriptions, meeting schedules and locations, official website/social links, officer contact details, and photo galleries.
* **Smart Filtering:** Browse beyond basic alphabetical lists. Search and filter organizations by multiple interest areas (e.g., Athletics, Fine Arts, Tech, Community Service, Cultural).

### 2. Tailored User Experiences

* **UH ID Authentication:** Secure login using your official UH credentials.
* **Custom Notifications:** Define your personal interest areas in your user profile to get notified whenever a new matching club is added or updated.

### 3. Dedicated Management Tools

* **Club Admin Controls:** Designated club officers can easily manage and update their club’s profile, photos, and meeting details in real time.
* **Super Admin Moderation:** Site administrators oversee platform safety, approve privileges, monitor content appropriateness, and create new interest categories.

## Future Enhancements

After building the site with its foundational features, we plan to look into adding the following enhancements:

* **RIO Spreadsheet Sync:** Bulk importing and updating of official university club data via CSV integrations.
* **Automated Listing Expirations:** A seasonal renewal system (per semester or academic year) requiring club admins to re-verify active status, ensuring stale listings are automatically pruned.
* **Content Change Alerts:** Automatic notifications sent to Super Admins whenever club details change to maintain site integrity.
* **Student Ratings & Reviews:** Peer feedback to help incoming students gauge club culture and activity levels.
